## Sound

Sound can be make with ```sound(<sound>)```

With sound of insect, wind, jazz, metal, east, crow, casio, space, numbers, ...

Multiple samples can be used : ```sound(<sound>:<sample_number>)```

*For instance:*

```js
sound("casio:1")
```
